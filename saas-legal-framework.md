## 2. End User License Agreement (EULA)
To be displayed upon first login for School Admins:
- **Data Ownership:** The school retains ownership of their student data.
- **Service Availability:** 99.9% uptime SLA via Cloudflare.
- **Fair Use:** Strict prohibition against automated API scraping.
- **Termination:** We reserve the right to suspend accounts due to missed subscription payments or AUP violations.

## 3. Subscription & Refund Policy
- **Trials:** 14-day free trial; no credit card required.
- **Refunds:** Strict no-refund policy post-trial completion.
- **Non-Payment:** 7-day grace period before account suspension via `SubscriptionMiddleware`.

## 4. Digital Watermarking Architecture
To protect your brand, all PDF/Excel exports generated by schools will automatically include:
1. **Header Logo:** The individual school's logo.
2. **Footer Copyright:** `Generated securely by codebyvishu.in SaaS ERP | codebyvishu.in`
3. **Audit Meta:** Invisible metadata inside the PDF tracking the `User ID` and `Timestamp` to trace data leaks.
